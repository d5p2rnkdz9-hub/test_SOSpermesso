<context>
The rinnovo tree is simpler than conversione because it's mostly a lookup: select permit → show renewal method + docs. The complexity is in the permit sub-types (especially famiglia with 10 variants). The Notion DB is the source of truth for renewal rules, docs, and warnings.

The conversione tree was hand-authored with carefully crafted outcome text. The rinnovo results are generated from Notion data with a `buildResult()` helper that formats method, docs, and warnings into TreeNode sections. This means re-running the fetch script updates all rinnovo content automatically.

- `scripts/fetch-rinnovo-data.ts` — Notion fetch script
- `src/lib/rinnovo-notion-data.generated.ts` — generated data (41 permits)
